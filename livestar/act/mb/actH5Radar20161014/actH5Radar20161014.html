BEWARE OF TROLLS

TIPS ON HOW TO DEAL WITH
UNWANTED COMMENTS

We’ve recently got word of trolls logging on and saying some pretty harsh stuff. Here are a few ways you can protect yourself against unwanted comments.

  • MUTE

    stop a viewer from commenting on your stream by tapping their profile. Then tap the info button on the upper right side and choose mute. Muted viewers can still watch, but can’t comment.

  • BLOCK

    block anyone from viewing your stream by tap-ping their profile and tapping the info button on the upper right side. Choose block to stop them from viewing your streams.

  • REPORT

    if a streamers content is offensive, tap on their profile, then tap the info button on the upper right side of the screen and choose report.

On a serious note, we want all of our users to have the best experience possible. Help us by using the mute, block or report button within the app. And be nice to each other - our community is strong, but we are stronger when we are unified. Lets make Radar.tv a safe place together.

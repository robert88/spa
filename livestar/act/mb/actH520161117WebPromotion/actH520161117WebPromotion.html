Great news folks! You can now watch and interact in real-time with your favorite streamers through your computer! Livestar has launched its web version, allowing you to have your daily dosage of awesomeness rom any device!

Apart from having all the features that you already love in the app, the desktop version comes with a few extra perks:

  • Rewatch or catch up with the latest live streams from your favorite streamers.

  • Recharge your Livestar account through PayPal.

  • Friendly interface with a broad range of live streams to choose from.

  • Receive recommendations based on your activity.

To access Livestar for web, you can use the same username/email and password chosen upon registration for the app.
